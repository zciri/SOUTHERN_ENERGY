The Solar Process

Made easy by the experts at Southern Energy

Step 1. Free consultation with one of our experts:

By requesting a free quote from one of our experts, we will take the information you provide and convert it into a solar proposal that is created exclusively for you. Our proposals are reviewed by solar experts in order to save you the most amount of money possible.

Step 2. Engineering a solar system uniquely for your home:

After the experts at Southern Energy are finished with your proposal, they will send it to you and await feedback. If all is well with it, we will proceed with engineering a solar system that is designed uniquely for your home. Decisions about add-ons will be made during this part of the process and the experts at Southern Energy will make it a painless process for you.

In this same step, an expert solar inspector will come and survey your home to see if the design we've made is going to be the best fit. If changes need to be made, an expert will contact you immediately to let you know what the best course of action would be.

Step 3. City Permits:

Once all finalizations are made to your solar system on paper, Southern Energy will send off the information to the city to get approval. This process usually only takes a week or two, meaning you can have your solar panel process concluded quickly.

Step 4. Installation of your Solar Power System:

After all else is concluded, the installation of your solar system and applicable add-ons will begin. You'll soon be free from depending on power companies! You'll be living off of clean energy all while hugely reducing your carbon footprint.
